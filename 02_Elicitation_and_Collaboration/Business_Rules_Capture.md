**Project:** [From BA_Project_Checkpoint.md]
**Last Updated:** [Date]

> **Purpose:** Catalogue every business rule identified during elicitation sessions. Business rules constrain, trigger, or enable business decisions and processes. They feed directly into requirements specification in KA5 and governance validation in KA3.
>
> **How to use with the agent:** Say "Log a business rule: [description]" and the agent will classify and append it to the correct section.
>
> **Rule Types:**
> - **Definitional** — defines a term, threshold, or classification (e.g., "A contract exceeding €50,000 requires CFO sign-off")
> - **Behavioural** — governs how a process must execute (e.g., "Revenue may not be recognised until the client acceptance form is received")
> - **Derivation** — calculates or derives a value (e.g., "Monthly recognised revenue = Total Contract Value ÷ Contract Duration in months")
> - **Constraint** — prohibits an action or state (e.g., "Batch recognition override is not permitted under IFRS 15")
